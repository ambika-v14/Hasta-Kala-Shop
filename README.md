🎨 Hasta Kala Shop
An AI-powered Android application built for small artisans and self-employed craft sellers to manage sales, inventory, income tracking, and product analytics.

📱 Project Overview
Hasta Kala Shop helps artisans who sell handmade products like banana fiber bags, baskets, and keychains.

Small business owners often struggle to:

Track daily sales
Identify best-selling products
Manage inventory
Reduce unsold stock
Monitor income growth
This application provides a smart and easy-to-use mobile solution for managing handcrafted product businesses.
